Login information

The link is located on the top-right corner of the site. Your login has been sent on your mail box by the Administrator, it must correspond to a valid email address

If you have problem to login into the website please contact the Administrator at lostpassword@liloquiz.com

After you are logged in, you have access to your personal information, in the panel menu.

Profile Administrator

An administrator can manage all data of the website by clicking on the "Admin" link.

He can manage data of :
  • Exams
  • GroupTypes
  • Questions
  • Quizzes
  • Users

Profile Examiner

An examiner can create new exam for many candidates.

To assign exam, the examiner must have some credits. It is possible to add credits in the "Account parameters".

For this Free Version, the credit is unlimited.

To create and assign an exam to a (future) candidate, it must define :

  • The candidate information
  • The quiz for this exam

After the exam is created and paid, the candidate will receive an email with login information. He have 24 hours to pass the exam until it was canceled. After the candidate has finished his exam, the examiner will received an email with the link of the final result.

The examiner can search the exam he has assign to (and only this), he can see the progress of it

Profile Manager

The manager can create quizzes and questions usable by all examiners.

For each question, he must define :

  • The title
  • The groupType of the question
  • The difficulty
  • The number of second available for the question
  • The explanation
  • The type of response (single choice, multiple choice, full text)
  • The available and right(s) response

A difficult question allows to earn more points for the final result, '1 star' is an easy question and '5 stars' is a very difficult question.

For each quiz, he must define :

  • The title
  • The description
  • The groupTypes of the questions

The difficulty of the quiz is the average of its questions difficulty.

The time available of the quiz is calculated with its questions times.

Profile Candidate

A candidate pass a given exam that has been assigned by an examiner. An email must have been send on an valid email address.

The first step is to log in with the temporary password given in the email.

After this step, the exam begin, the candidate has a timer display on the screen. This is the remaining time to finish the quiz.

After he finished the quiz, the exam is validated and the examiner received the result of the exam.
